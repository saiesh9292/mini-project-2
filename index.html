About Elite Interiors

Crafting beautiful spaces since 1995

Our Story

Founded in 2019 By Saiesh Patil, Elite Interiors began as a small family business with a simple mission: to create beautiful, functional furniture that transforms houses into homes. What started in a modest workshop has grown into a leading furniture retailer, but our commitment to quality and craftsmanship remains unchanged.

We believe that furniture is more than just objects in a room – it's the foundation of your daily life, the backdrop to your memories, and the expression of your personal style. That's why every piece in our collection is carefully selected for its quality, design, and ability to enhance your living space.

Our Mission

To provide premium furniture solutions that combine exceptional craftsmanship, innovative design, and sustainable practices, making luxury accessible to everyone while maintaining the highest standards of customer service.

Our Values

  • Quality First: We never compromise on materials or craftsmanship
  • Customer Focus: Your satisfaction is our top priority
  • Sustainability: We're committed to environmentally responsible practices
  • Innovation: We constantly evolve to meet changing needs
